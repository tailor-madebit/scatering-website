What We Do

Welcome

Are you craving for that delicious Nigerian homemade soup with fresh vegetables and mouthwatering ingredients? Stephen's kitchen is your one stop shop for your richly nutritious and favorite Nigerian soups and stews hence our motto: Stephen's for yor comfort.

Stephen's kitchen exist to offer busy individuals tasty and home-made soups. we offer a wide variety of affordable and richly made soups garnished with all sort of proteins and freshly sorted condiments. We are aware of your time constraint from busy life schedule, work stress, traffic congestion delays; hence, we bridge that gap with all the delicacies you will need to feel at home and relax. Additionally, to avoid eating out, spending big and also potentially having to eat manufactured foods thereby setting back your fitness goals, we hygienically provide you the option of eating what you know and love, we also take custom orders if you're on a special diet.
